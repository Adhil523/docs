# Formance Ledger

## What is Formance Ledger?

Formance Ledger lets you model and record complex money movements, helping you accelerate your business operations. Build financial dashboards, deliver payments to your marketplace partners, and accurately track revenue across your entire operation.

## I found a bug!

Did you find a bug in Formance Ledger? That's great! We need your help to make it better. You can [file a bug report](https://github.com/formancehq/ledger/issues/new?assignees=&labels=bug&template=bug_report.md&title=) and tell us all about it.

## Formance Ledger is Open Source

Formance Ledger is [Open Source](https://github.com/formancehq/ledger/blob/main/LICENSE), and we welcome your contributions, whether it's code or working on these very documents. We maintain a welcoming and inclusive community: [Join us on GitHub](https://github.com/formancehq/ledger) and learn how to get involved.
